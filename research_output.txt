Africa's Population Overview:

The current population of Africa is approximately 1.49 billion people as of 2024. Africa is the second most populous continent after Asia. The continent has experienced rapid population growth over the past century due to improved healthcare, declining infant mortality rates, and high fertility rates.

Key demographic trends:
- High population growth rate
- Young population structure with median age under 20
- Significant urban population growth
- Diverse ethnic and cultural composition

Population distribution varies significantly across regions, with the most populous countries being Nigeria, Ethiopia, Egypt, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
